Do you find yourself stretched thin on time and wondering how the weeks and years fly by, 
but you didn’t get to spend as much time on the things, or with the people, you love? 
Focusing on the method to improve life efficiency, this site is about sharing information 
and insight into a variety of topics that can help optimize your busy lives.
From professional skills and financial literacy, to mental health and physical wellness, 
we here collect infos to help you learn how to live your best life. 

Tiers of Infos：
1. data acquisition 数据搜集（get rid of garbage）
2. info classification 信息分类（focus on key info 去掉非关键的信息）
3. concept extraction 概念提取（semantic objects extraction through meta methods）
4. knowledge building 知识构建（link to knowledge framework 与已有的知识框架建立链接）
5. system abstraction 系统抽象（work on the abstraction layer 在抽象的层面上与已有知识系统形成关联）

高级别要对低级别形成有效反馈，提升低级别信息处理的效率。
